The SAP Converged Cloud is a private cloud solution managed by SAP. It is an internal Infrastructure-as-a-Service platform deployed worldwide in 17 regions to support all of SAP’s cloud businesses on a global scale.

Challenge yourself in the world of cloud infrastructure and become an integral part of the group that directs SAP’s strategy to move to the Cloud. Work in a globally diverse and innovative team where courage and originality are highly valued and for a company where you are encouraged to make the decision on which path your career should take you. We are open for home office or hybrid models in the Eastern and Central time zone.

The team offering this position is responsible for a fleet of globally distributed Kubernetes clusters. All software that makes up SAP’s Converged Cloud is hosted on this fleet. We are building services that allow us and our customers to leverage the power of Kubernetes more easily.

How You Might Contribute

We are looking for a developer who feels at home in the cloud-native ecosystem. You will be designing, implementing, testing, deploying, and monitoring cloud services and applications using the Kubernetes toolset.

Here are some things that we have worked on recently:
  • A controller that drains a Kubernetes node when the VM state changes in vSphere
  • Annotated Helm charts with metadata to fire dynamically created Prometheus alerts
  • Automated certificate renewal for Kubernetes Ingress resources
  • Implemented a centralized version and change tracking for Concourse CI
Educational And Qualifications
  • Experience in agile practices and test-driven development
  • Fluent in at least one of Golang, Python, Ruby, Javascript
  • Hands-on experience with Docker and Kubemetes
  • Degree in Applied Sciences. Preferred field of study: Computer Science or related field
  • Excellent abstract problem-solving skills, high level of commitment and reliability, good communication skills, fun working in a multinational team, understanding the dynamics of communication in open-source projects.
